Project 3

Continuing from Project 3

The theme I chose deals with the video game "Halo 4" and providing other players with vital information for missions throughout the game.

I find the story within most games is what captivates it's intended audience, and thats what keeps the player involved in continuous gameplay.

The life of the game gains new meaning after you have understood the story and have attached yourself to it's theme.

I want to provide the next player with just enough vital information to not spoil the players experience,but enhance it and cause the player to feel prepared for the upcoming missions.

Javascript explanations:

Ordinance Request are custom weapon combinations that a player can choose from

Spartans are the soldiers you can play as in "Halo 4"

Planet Requiem is the location where the game takes place

Promethean Knight is the enemy alien species
